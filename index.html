About

Hello!

I am a very driven person, actively seeking to fulfill my dream of working with two things I am passionate about: language and technology.

I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Linguistics from Universidade Federal da Bahia, in Brazil, and a Master's degree in Computational Linguistics from Montclair State University, in the US.

Currently, I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the New York Institute of Technology. I am part of the Health Informatics and Data Analytics Lab, under guidance of Dr. Huanying Gu and Dr. Jerry Cheng.
